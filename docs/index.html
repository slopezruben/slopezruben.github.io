I have worked in...

Hackató Robotech

Part of the winning team of the Hackató Robotech in Tarragona. Our goal was to design a robotic system to help clean up plastic waste from Tarragona's beaches.

We proposed using the existing 5G towers along the Catalan coast to control a fleet of GPS-equipped robots, along with a prototype to show for it.

Our setup allowed for real-time coordination and remote management, making the system efficient, autonomous, and scalable.

SayITEasy

Developed SayITEasy, winner of the Berri UP Award at the Hackathon #OdiseIA4Good.

It uses AI to make information easier to understand for people with cognitive or reading difficulties, with the goal to promote digital inclusion through ethical and accessible technology.
